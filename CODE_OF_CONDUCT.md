# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This is a community project for Sacramento AI builders. Everyone is welcome regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Standards

- Be direct and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Disagree on ideas, not people
- Keep it relevant to AI and the Sacramento community

## Not Welcome

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Spam or self-promotion unrelated to AI
- Content that demeans any group of people

## Enforcement

Issues can be reported by opening a GitHub issue or contacting the maintainer directly. Violations may result in removal from the project.

This project uses a simplified code of conduct. If you need the full Contributor Covenant, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org/
